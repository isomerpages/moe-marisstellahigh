<p><strong>The Badminton CCA aims to:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Deepen the students’ passion for the sport;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Forge strong friendships and a sense of camaraderie among teammates;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build a sense of belonging and pride for the school; and&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop qualities of a Sportsman to be cooperative, humble, gracious and
honourable.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Marists are given the opportunities to participate in the following competitions:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>‘B’ and ‘C’ Division North Zone Badminton Championships&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>‘B’ and ‘C’ Division National Badminton Championships</p>
</li>
</ul>
