<p><strong>The Outdoor Activities Club (ODAC) CCA aims to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Help members to acquire and improve their knowledge and skills in Bouldering;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provides students with targeted training for individual growth; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inculcate leadership, respect, resilience and teamwork.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Marists are given the opportunities to participate in the following competitions:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Inter-School Bouldering Championship ‘B’ Division</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inter-School Bouldering Championship ‘C' Division</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ignite Boulder Competition</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Spark Boulder Competition</p>
</li>
</ul>
