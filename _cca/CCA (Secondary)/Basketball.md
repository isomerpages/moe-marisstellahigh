<p><strong>The Basketball CCA aims to:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Instil important values such as teamwork, discipline and resilience;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cultivate a fast-paced and competitive playing style; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Nurture students into athletes who hold themselves to high standards on
and off the court.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Marists are given the opportunities to participate in the following competitions:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;National School Games&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;BAS Youth Cup</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;External Tournaments (eg. ARSJC Cup, Raffles Cup)**</p>
</li>
</ul>
