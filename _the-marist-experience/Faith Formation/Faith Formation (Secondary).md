<h1>Faith Formation</h1>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:75%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Faith%20Formation/Religious%20Education.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong>The Faith Formation Programme aims to:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Develop Marists to be men with deep spirituality; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop Marists to be men with a strong belief in the doctrines of a religion,
based on spiritual conviction rather than proof.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>To achieve these goals, the following programmes are offered:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><u>School-Wide Activities</u></strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bilingual Morning Prayers led by student prayer leaders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Commitment Liturgy at the start of year</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 1 Catholic Camp (open to all Sec 1 students)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lent Programme – Stations of the Cross Morning Prayers, Maundy Thursday
Observance and Sec 4 Agape Meal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Easter Programme – Easter Morning Prayers and Easter Monday Celebrations
for Staff &amp; Students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 4 Spiritual Preparation for the GCE ‘O’ Level Examinations – 9-Day
Novena and Commitment Mass</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Founder’s Day Week Celebrations – 9-day Novena to St Marcellin Champagnat
morning prayers followed by Founder’s Day Mass</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>Catholic Activities</u></strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Masses on Days of Obligation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Penitential Service</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Year-End Catholic Activities Camp</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>信仰培育课程的宗旨在于：</u></strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>培育海星学子成为具有深厚精神修养的人；</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>培养拥有坚定宗教信念的海星学子，以精神信念为根基。为实现这些目标，学校提供以下课程与活动：全校活动</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>双语晨祷 —— 由学生领袖带领</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>学年初承诺礼（Commitment Liturgy）</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>中一天主教营 —— 面向所有中一学生</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>四旬期活动 —— 苦路晨祷、圣周四仪式及中四“爱宴”（Agape Meal）</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>复活节活动 —— 复活节晨祷及复活节星期一庆祝活动</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>中四备考灵修 —— “九日敬礼”仪式（Novena）及信念弥撒</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>校庆 —— 连续九天的“九日敬礼”仪式（Novena）以纪念圣玛塞林·尚巴尼亚（St Marcellin Champagnat）及校庆弥撒
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>

</p>
<ul>
<li>

</li>

<p>忏悔礼</p>
