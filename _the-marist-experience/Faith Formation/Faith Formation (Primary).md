<p><strong>The Faith Formation Programme aims to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Deepen the Catholic faith of Marists; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contribute to the moral and social development of Marists.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>To achieve these goals, the following programmes are offered:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bilingual Morning Prayers led by Student Prayer Leaders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lent Programme – Penitential Service</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Founder’s Day Celebrations – 9-day Novena in honour of St Marcellin Champagnat
and Founder’s Day Mass</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weekly RE Lessons</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Tuesdays: P1 and P4 students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wednesdays: P2 and P5 students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thursdays: P3 and P6 students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fridays: Praise and Worship sessions or First Friday masses in the chapel.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>信仰培育课程（Faith Formation Programme）</strong> 的宗旨在于：</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>加深海星学子的天主教信仰；</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>促进他们在道德与社会层面的全面成长。
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>为达成这些目标，学校特别开展以下活动与课程：</p>
<ul>

<p>双语晨祷 —— 由学生领袖带领</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>四旬期 活动—— 忏悔礼</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>校庆 —— 连续九天的“九日敬礼”仪式以纪念圣玛塞林·尚巴尼亚及校庆弥撒
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>每周宗教课</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>周二：小一及小四学生
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>周三：小二及小五学生
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>周四：小三及小六学生
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>周五: 赞美与敬拜，或在教堂举行的圣心弥撒</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
